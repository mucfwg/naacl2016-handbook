Abstract#==#The paper focuses on the creation of a semantic-based hybrid Machine
Translation system between Bulgarian and English in the domain of Information
Technology. The preprocessing strategies are presented. A method for the
substitution of English word forms with the synsets or Bulgarian representative
lemmas is discussed. Finally, the creation of a factored model in the Moses
system is described.
