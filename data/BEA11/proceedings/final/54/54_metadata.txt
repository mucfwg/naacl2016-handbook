Abstract#==#This study describes the design of the NTNU-YZU system for the automated
evaluation of scientific writing shared task. We employ a convolutional neural
network with the Word2Vec/GloVe embedding representation to predict whether a
sentence needs language editing. For the Boolean prediction track, our best
F-score of 0.6108 ranked second among the ten submissions. Our system also
achieved an F-score of 0.7419 for the probabilistic estimation track, ranking
fourth among the nine submissions.
