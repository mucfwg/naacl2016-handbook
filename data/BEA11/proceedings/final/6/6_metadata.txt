Abstract#==#We investigate how users of intelligent writing assistance tools deal with
correct, incorrect, and incomplete feedback. To this end, we conduct an
empirical user study around an L1 text revision task for German. Our
participants should revise stylistic issues in two given texts using a novel
web-based writing environment that highlights potential issues and provides
corresponding feedback messages. In comparison to a control group, we find that
precision plays a more important role than recall, which confirms previous
findings for other languages, issue types, user groups, and experimental
setups.
