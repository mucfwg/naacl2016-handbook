Abstract#==#When automatically detecting deception, it is important to model individual
differences across speakers.  We explore the automatic identification of
individual traits such as gender, native language, and personality, using
acoustic-prosodic and lexical features from an initial non-deceptive dialogue. 
We also explore predicting success at deception and at deception detection,
using the same features.
