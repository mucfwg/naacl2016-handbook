Abstract#==#In this paper, we present Farasa, a fast and accurate Arabic segmenter. Our
approach is based on SVM-rank using linear kernels. We measure the performance
of the segmenter in terms of accuracy and efficiency, in two NLP tasks, namely
Machine Translation (MT) and Information Retrieval (IR). Farasa outperforms or
equalizes state-of-the-art Arabic segmenters (Stanford and MADAMIRA), while
being more than one order of magnitude faster.
