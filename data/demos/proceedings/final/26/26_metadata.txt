Abstract#==#We demonstrate the Task Completion Platform (TCP); a multi-domain dialogue
platform that can host and execute large numbers of goal-orientated dialogue
tasks. The platform features a task configuration language, TaskForm, that
allows the definition of each individual task to be decoupled from the
overarching dialogue policy used by the platform to complete those tasks. This
separation
allows for simple and rapid authoring of new tasks, while dialogue policy and
platform functionality evolve independent of the tasks. The current platform
includes machine learnt models that provide contextual slot carry-over,
flexible item selection, and task selection/switching. Any new task immediately
gains the benefit of these pieces of built-in platform functionality. The
platform
is used to power many of the multi-turn dialogues supported by the Cortana
personal assistant.
