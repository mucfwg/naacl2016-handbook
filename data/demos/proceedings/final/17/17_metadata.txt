Abstract#==#In this paper, we use multilingual Natural Language Processing (NLP) tools to
improve the reading experience of parallel texts on mobile devices. Such
enterprise poses multiple challenging issues both from the NLP and from the
Human Computer Interaction (HCI) perspectives. We discuss these problems and
report on our own solutions, now implemented in a full-fledge bilingual reading
device.
