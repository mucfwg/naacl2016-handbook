Abstract#==#This paper describes the SAARSHEFF systems that participated in the English
Semantic Textual Similarity (STS) task in SemEval-2016. We extend the work on
using machine translation (MT) metrics in the STS task by annotating the STS
datasets with a variety of MT scores for each pair of text snippets in the STS
datasets. We trained our systems using boosted tree ensembles and achieved
competitive results that outperforms the median Pearson correlation scores from
all participating systems.
