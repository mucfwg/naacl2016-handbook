Abstract#==#This paper describes the results of the participation of The University of
Melbourne in the community question-answering (CQA) task of SemEval 2016 (Task
3-B). We obtained a MAP score of 70.20 on the test set, by combining three
classifiers: a NaiveBayes classifier and a support vector machine (SVM) each
trained over lexical similarity features, and a convolutional neural network
(CNN). The CNN uses word embeddings and machine translation evaluation scores
as features.
