Abstract#==#This paper describes our participation in the SemEval-2016 Task 1: Semantic
Textual Similarity (STS). We developed three methods for the English subtask
(STS Core). The first method is unsupervised and uses WordNet and word2vec to
measure a token-based overlap. In our second approach, we train a neural
network on two features. The third method uses word2vec and LDA with regression
splines.
