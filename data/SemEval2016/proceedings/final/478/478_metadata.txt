Abstract#==#In this paper, we describe the system developed for our participation in the
Clinical TempEval task of SemEval 2016 (task 12). Our team focused on the
subtasks of span and attribute identification and proposed a system that
integrates both statistical and linguistic approaches. Our system is based on
Conditional Random Fields with high-precision linguistic features.
