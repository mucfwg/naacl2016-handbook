Abstract#==#This paper details the participation of ISTI-CNR to task 4 of Semeval 2016.
Among the five subtasks, special attention has been paid to the five-point
scale quantification subtask.
The quantification method we propose for this subtask is based on a standard
document-by-document regression method, to which a compensation for error
caused by the bias of the model towards high prevalence labels is applied in
order to produce the quantification estimates.
