Abstract#==#In this paper, we describe our participation in the first shared task on
automated stance  detection (SemEval 2016 Task 6). We consider the task as a
multidimensional classification
problem and thus use a sequence of stacked classifiers. For subtask A, we
utilize a rich
feature set that does not rely on external information such as additional
tweets or knowledge
bases. For subtask B, we rely on the similarity of tweets in this task with
tweets from subtask
A in order to transfer the models learnt in subtask A.
