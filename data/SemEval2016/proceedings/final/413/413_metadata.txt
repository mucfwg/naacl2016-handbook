Abstract#==#This paper reports the IIT-TUDA participation in the SemEval 2016 shared task 5
of Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA) for subtask 1. We describe our system
incorporating domain dependency graph features, distributional thesaurus and
unsupervised lexical induction using an unlabeled external corpus for aspect
based sentiment analysis. Overall, we submitted 29 runs, covering 7 languages
and 4 different domains. Our system is placed first in sentiment polarity
classification for the English laptop domain, Spanish and Turkish restaurant
reviews, and opinion target expression for Dutch and French in restaurant
domain, and scores in medium ranks for aspect category identification and
opinion target extraction.
