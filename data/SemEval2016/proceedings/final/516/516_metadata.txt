Abstract#==#This paper describes our system for the detection of stances in tweets
submitted to SemEval 2016 Task 6A. The system uses an ensemble of learning
algorithms, fine-tuned using a genetic algorithm. We experiment with various
off-the-shelf classifiers and build our model using standard lexical and a
number of task-specific features. Our system ranked 3rd among the 19 systems
submitted to this task.
