Abstract#==#This paper contains the description of our participation at task 4 (sub-task A,
Message Polarity  Classification)  of  SemEval-2016.   Our proposed   system  
consists   mainly   of                                             three steps. 
Firstly,
the
preprocessing
step
includes
the  tokenization  and                                            identification     
         
        of 

special
elements
including URLs,
hashtags, user mentions and emoticons.                                            The
second step
aims
at
selecting
 and 
extracting  the  feature  set.                                             Finally, a
supervised
approach, in particular a Support Vector Machine has been applied to tackle the
classification problem.
