Abstract#==#We describe the systems we have used for participating in Subtasks D (binary
quantification) and E (ordinal quantification) of SemEval-2016 Task 4
``Sentiment Analysis in Twitter''. The binary quantification system uses a
``Probabilistic Classify and Count'' (PCC) approach that leverages the
calibrated probabilities obtained from the output of an SVM. The ordinal 
quantification approach uses an ordinal tree of PCC binary quantifiers, where
the tree is generated via a splitting criterion that minimizes the ordinal
quantification loss.
