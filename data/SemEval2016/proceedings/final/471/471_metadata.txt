Abstract#==#In this paper, we describe the KULeuven-LIIR system at the Clinical TempEval
2016 Shared Task for the narrative container relation sub-task (CR). Our
approach is based on the cTAKES Temporal system (Lin et al, 2015). We explored
extending this system with different features. Moreover, we provide an error
analysis of the submitted system, and report on some additional experiments
done after submission.
