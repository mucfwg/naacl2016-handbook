Abstract#==#In this paper, we present our contribution
in SemEval2016 task7: Determining Sentiment
Intensity of English and Arabic Phrases,
where we use web search engines for English
and Arabic unsupervised sentiment intensity
prediction. Our work is based, first, on a
group of classic sentiment lexicons (e.g. Sentiment140
Lexicon, SentiWordNet). Second,
on web search engines’ ability to find the cooccurrence
of sentences with predefined negative
and positive words. The use of web search
engines (e.g. Google Search API) enhance the
results on phrases built from opposite polarity
terms.
