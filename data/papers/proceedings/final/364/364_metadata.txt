Abstract#==#We address the task of annotating images with semantic tuples. Solving this
problem requires an algorithm able to deal with hundreds of classes for each
argument of the tuple. In such contexts, data sparsity becomes a key challenge.
We propose handling this by incorporating feature representations of both the
inputs (images) and outputs (argument classes) into a factorized log-linear
model.
