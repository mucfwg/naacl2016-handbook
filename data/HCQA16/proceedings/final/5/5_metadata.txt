Abstract#==#This work discusses a mix of challenges arising from Watson Discovery Advisor
(WDA), an industrial strength descendant of the Watson Jeopardy! Question
Answering system currently used in production in industry settings. Typical
challenges include generation of appropriate training questions, adaptation to
new industry domains, and iterative improvement of the system through manual
error analyses.
