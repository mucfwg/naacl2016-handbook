Abstract#==#This paper describes the contribution of LT3 for the CLPsych 2016 Shared Task
on automatic triage of mental health forum posts. Our systems use multiclass
Support Vector Machines (SVM), cascaded binary SVMs and ensembles with a rich
feature set. The best systems obtain macro-averaged F-scores of 40% on the full
task and 80% on the green versus alarming distinction. Multiclass SVMs with all
features score best in terms of F-score, whereas feature filtering with
bi-normal separation and classifier ensembling are found to improve recall of
alarming posts.
