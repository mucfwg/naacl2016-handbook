Abstract#==#This paper presents a system capable of performing automatic triage of forum
posts from ReachOut.com, a mental health online forum. The system assigns to
each post a tag that indicates how urgently moderator attention is needed. The
evaluation is based on experiments conducted on the CLPsych 2016 task, and the
system is released as an open-source software.
