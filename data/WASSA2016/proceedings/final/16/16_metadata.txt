Abstract#==#This paper investigates the effect of various types of linguistic features
(lexical, syntactic and semantic) for training classifiers to detect threats of
violence in a corpus of YouTube comments. Our results show that combinations of
lexical features outperform the use of more complex syntactic and semantic
features for this task.
